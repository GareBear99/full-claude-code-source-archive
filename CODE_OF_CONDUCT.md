# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Please keep participation respectful, direct, and constructive.

Examples of positive behavior:

- reporting issues clearly and reproducibly
- giving specific, actionable feedback
- respecting boundaries around unofficial/archive framing
- focusing discussion on docs, tooling, packaging, and repository quality

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- spam or deliberately disruptive behavior
- misrepresenting the project as an official upstream release
- posting secrets, private data, or exploit details publicly

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove content, close discussions, or block participation that harms the project or community.
